OUR VISION
World Tourism is aimed at taking investment transparency, globalization, and decentralization to the next level by implementing blockchain technology in the tourism sector. We are creating a solid bridge between the blockchain universe and the tourism world by providing a transparent and legally compliant framework for tokenized asset structuring and operation. The objective is to be an end-to-end encrypted system with zero-knowledge technology over which communications, data, or value can be securely exchanged, transmitted and stored. It is an integration of revolutionary technologies that create a system over which innovation can accelerate by combining four functions that are traditionally done separately:
1) Transactions
2) Communication,
3) Security and
4) Competitive governance.
This is done in a secure and anonymous manner, using a worldwide distributed blockchain and computing infrastructure. The system integrates multiple best-in-class technologies to form an open platform for permission-less innovation that can evolve with user preferences.
We are helping to expand and explore the boundaries of tourism and cryptocurrency possibilities, and encourage industry experts to recognize blockchain tools as the new reality in classical tourism markets. Ultimately, World Tourism aims to be the benchmark for tokenized tourism assets and vehicles that are invested and incorporated in traditional tourism instruments as well as in emerging distributed economy opportunities.
